About Ritik


Hi! I am currently a freshman at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences! Growing up with a public education, I never knew what field I would end up studying, but after participating in the activities and opportunities available to me, I was able to discover my passion for innovation. Aside from my love for engineering, I enjoyed participating in many extracurriculars. Dance is one of my biggest passions as I learned about leadership and teaching as a choreographer. I also played the Alto Saxophone as a soloist, earned a Black Belt, 2nd degree in TaeKwonDo, and competed in business competitions. As the Vice President of the class of 2018, American-Indo Student Association Co-President, and JUMP Dance Club Vice President in high school, I worked hard to leave my legacy on high school, and take the skills I learned to college. I also have work experience from BlueStamp Engineering as a Junior Instructor. I worked with over 40 high school students to teach them electrical, mechanical, and software engineering through individual, unique projects. In addition, I have worked at Math Enrichment at Prospect High School as a Pre-Algebra and Algebra 2/Trigonometry teacher aid.
